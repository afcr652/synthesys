## What Does Synthesys Actually Do?

Think of it as a video production suite where the "actors" are AI avatars and the "voice talent" is a library of 1,000+ ultra-realistic voices. You write a script, pick an avatar, hit generate, and a few minutes later you have a video.

But it's grown well past that basic pitch. Here's what's actually on the platform right now:

**AI Video with Avatars** — Pick from 200+ stock avatars or create your own digital twin. Customize expressions, add product placement, generate UGC-style videos for ads. The lip sync is genuinely impressive.

**AI Voice Generator** — 1,000+ voices across 175+ languages and dialects. Voice cloning is included on paid plans, so you can replicate your own voice for consistent branding.

**AI Dubbing & Video Translation** — Upload an existing video, pick a target language, and Synthesys re-dubs it. Useful if you're trying to reach international audiences without re-filming anything.

**Google VEO3 & Sora2 Integration** — Higher plans include credits for these cutting-edge AI video generation models, which is a genuinely nice extra.

**Speech-to-Speech, Faceswap, Talking Photos** — A bunch of features that sound gimmicky but are actually useful once you're deep in a content workflow.

The whole thing runs in-browser, so Mac, PC, whatever — it doesn't matter.
